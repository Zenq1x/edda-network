Why Edda

Built different.
By design.

Core Protocol

Proof of History

Cryptographic timestamps embedded in every block. Validators agree on transaction order without gossip rounds — enabling true 65,000 TPS without sacrificing decentralization.

65,000 TPS
Consensus

Tower BFT

Byzantine fault-tolerant finality in under 400ms. Transactions are irreversible before most users finish blinking.

Smart Contracts

WebAssembly

Deploy contracts in any language that compiles to WASM. Fuel-based gas metering keeps execution safe and predictable.

Economics

Hard Cap + Burn

500M EDDA maximum. 50% of every fee is destroyed permanently. The network becomes more scarce as it grows.

Networking

libp2p P2P

Gossipsub + Kademlia DHT + mDNS autodiscovery. Nodes find each other and sync automatically with no configuration.

Get Started

Try it in 60 seconds.

01

Open the Wallet

Go to wallet.eddachain.com and create a new wallet. Your keys stay in your browser — nothing is stored remotely.

02

Receive EDDA

Share your wallet address to receive EDDA. Every block rewards the validator who produced it with 10 EDDA.

03

Send a transaction

Paste any address, enter an amount, hit send. Watch it confirm on-chain in under 400ms.

04

Explore the chain

Open explorer.eddachain.com to see blocks, transactions, and accounts live.
